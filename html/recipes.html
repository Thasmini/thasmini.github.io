FOR THE GANACHE




Instruction

1. Grease and line an (8 inch) round form tin with baking paper

2. Break up the biscuits into 1-2cm pieces and set it aside

3.In a large bowl, melt together the butter, golden syrup, cocoa, cream and salt, in the microwave in 30 secons. Stir well between each until it’s just melted together. Don’t overheat.

4. Add the biscuit pieces and ⅔ of the maltesers to the chocolate mixture and mix well.

5. Tip into prepared tin, pressing down well

6. lace in the fridge for at least 3 hours.
