No Baking Chocolate Fidge Cake With Malteser Recipe

This easy No Baking Cake is mild and rich in flavour. It's recomended for people who have a sweet tooth and love chocolte.
Since my oven was broken I had to think of another way to make a cake for my project. That's when it hit me.
I remembered how we once made a frozen cake at school. I went online and found an amazing recipe.
For a beginner like me this cake was pretty easy to make. I followed the recipe step by step but also added my own touch to it.




FIRST STEPS

After work I went to buy the needed ingridients for my cake. I tried to find the least expensive irgidients.
With a total of 25.90 Euro I managed to buy everything I need and then I placed it on the table.




I prepared the necessary ingridients based on our recipe for an easy start.




First of all you should butter a backing paper on a round form tin. I forgot to record that step.
In a large bowl I added some butter and cut it smaller with a knife for easy melting.




Then I added some golden Syrup in to the bowl to give the mixture a caramell-like flavour.




I added enoug cacao powder and heated it up in a microwave for 30 seconds.




Every now and then I took the mixture out of the mircrowave and stirred it until it was smooth.




After that I added two cups of maltesers leaving some left for decorations.
I also added all the biscuits into the mix.




Then I poured the mixture in a round tin and tried to make the top flat.

THE GANACHE



I had to make heavy cream myself.
I added sugar to the cream and mixed it with a mixer.

Once the heavy cream was smooth and puffy I also added melted dark chocolate

The Ganacha was mixed by a handy mixer.

Then I poured the ganache over our mixture.
The more time passed the more holes and gap it had.

To cover up the holes I sprinkled some dark schocolate on top of our cake.
To make the cake more appealing I decided to decorate the cake with some matlesers.
For the finishing touch I also put some leftover dark chocolate in the middle of the cake.
